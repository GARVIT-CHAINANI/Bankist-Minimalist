Features

100% digital bank

Bankist is a **100% digital bank**—no branches, no paperwork, just powerful online banking at your fingertips. Open accounts, transfer money, track spending, and manage everything with ease. Fast, secure, and always accessible. Experience the future of banking, fully digital and built for you.

Watch your money grow

Watch your **money grow** with Bankist. Earn high interest on savings, track progress in real time, and enjoy smart insights that help you save more. Your goals, our tools—designed to make your money work harder. Start growing your wealth effortlessly with Bankist today.

Free debit card included

Get your **Bankist free debit card** with zero annual fees and instant activation. Enjoy secure payments, global ATM access, and real-time tracking. No paperwork, no delays—just fast, hassle-free banking. Perfect for everyday use, online shopping, and travel. Apply now and start spending smarter.
